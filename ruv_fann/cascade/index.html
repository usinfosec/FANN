Cascade Correlation Training for Dynamic Network Topology Optimization

This module implements the Cascade Correlation algorithm, which dynamically grows neural networks by adding hidden neurons one at a time. The algorithm alternates between training output weights and training candidate hidden neurons to maximize the correlation between candidate outputs and residual network errors.
